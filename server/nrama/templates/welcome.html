Note-o-rama

Add notes and highlights to any web page; organise and review your notes by source, author and #tag.

Graphic. Left panel: annotate any web page. Right panel: organise your notes.

Get it

Step 1: (optional) ***LINK sign up or ***LINK login.
(You don't need to sign up, but if you choose not to others may edit or delete your notes.)

Step 2: drag the link below to the bookmark bar in your browser (or otherwise bookmark it).

Click this link ***LINK to enable note-o-rama for this page.

  • Clicking highlighted text adds a note to the page.
  • You can review your notes *LINK here
  • If you come back to this page, notes will re-load when you ***LINK enable note-o-rama.

How to use it

When browsing a page, hit the bookmark you just made (step 2 above) to start making notes. (This bookmark won't take you to another page, it just injects some code.)

Clicking your user name on the top right of this page (***LINK or here) will take you to your notes.

Problems?

Note-o-rama should work in recent versions of Firefox, Chrome and Safari.

We don't promise that it will work in older browsers or in Internet Explorer.

Pro tips

Move notes by dragging any edge.

alt- or meta- click a quote to delete it.

Use #tags in notes (***LINK your tags).

Paste a bibtex entry into a note to organise by author (***LINK your authors).
