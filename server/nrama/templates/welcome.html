Note-o-rama

Add notes and highlights to any web page.
Organise and review your notes and quotes by source, author and #tag.

Figure. Left panel: annotate any web page. Right panel: organise your notes.

{>"registration.html"/}

How to use it

You can use Note-o-rama on any page you visit.

When browsing a page, hit the bookmark you just made (step 2 above) to start making notes. (This bookmark won't take you to another page, it just injects some code.)

NB: Your notes will be stored online, not in your browser. They are visible to all (like something you write on a blog or twitter).

{#userCtx} To see all your notes{^name} sign up or log in then{/name} click your user name on the top right of this page{?name} or here{/name}. {/userCtx}

Problems?

Note-o-rama should work in recent versions of Chrome, Firefox, Safari and Opera.
We don't promise that it will work in older browsers or Internet Explorer. Tant pis.

If it doesn't work, you may need to set your browser to accept cookies from all websites (sorry).

Issues may be reported here.

Tips

{#userCtx}
  • Move notes by dragging any edge.
  • To delete a note, delete all of its text (it will then disappear).
  • alt- or cmd- click a quote to delete it.
  • Use #tags in notes{?name} (your tags){/name}.
  • Paste a bibtex entry into a note to organise by author{?name} (your authors){/name}.
  • Hold shift, ctrl, meta or alt while selecting text if you don't want to quote (highlight) it.
{/userCtx}
