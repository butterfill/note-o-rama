How to use it

When browsing a page, hit the bookmark you just made to start making notes (it won't take you to another page, it just adds some code to the page you're on).

  • Selecting text will highlight it and add it to ***LINK your quotes.
  • Clicking a highlight will add a note to the page.
  • And any notes you made previously will re-load.

You can organise and edit your notes ***LINK here.

Pro tips

Use #tags in notes

Paste a bibtex entry into a note and note-o-rama will sort your notes by author
